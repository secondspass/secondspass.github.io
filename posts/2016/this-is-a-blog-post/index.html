This is a blog post

This is a blog post. The first in (hopefully) a series. A series of blog posts where I write about whatever is on my mind, probably mostly about computer stuff that I’m doing. An activity log, if you will. Maybe writing this is a way to organize my thoughts. Does it work? I don’t know! I only just started. I also find that announcing out loud that I’m going to do something severely decreases the chance of it ever getting done. Which is why I’m not going to say that I intend to write a blog post every other day.

But you just did!

I typed it. There’s a difference.

No there isn’t. Announcing out loud and posting on a public website are just two different forms of the same action!
